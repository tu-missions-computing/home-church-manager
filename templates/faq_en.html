How do I add a person to a home group?

Click on the "Members" tab on the navigation bar, and then click "Add Member" at the top of the page.


How do I take attendance?

Click on "Attendance" tab on the navigation bar. Select the current date and time and click "Take Attendance". Click the check boxes next to a person's name if they are present. Leave them blank if they absent.


How do I edit/view past attendance reports?

On the "Attendance" page, click on "View Attendance History." Click "Edit" for the report you would like to view.


How do I edit my home group information?

Click on the "Edit Home Group" tab and edit your home group information using the form.


How do I email my home group?

Click on the "Members" tab. On the top right, click the "Contact Members" button, compose, and send your email.

How do I add a person to a home group?

Click on the "Home Groups" tab on the navigation bar. Select the "Members" button of the desired home group. Click "Add Member" at the top of the page.


How do I delete a home group?

Click on the "Home Groups" tab. Select the "Delete" button of the desired home group. This will temporarily deactivate the home group. To reactivate a home group, click on "Show Inactive Home Groups" at the top of the page. Select "Reactivate" on the desired home group.


How do I view all members of all home groups?

Click on "Members" tab. Here, you can edit, create, or remove a member.


How do I search by member demographics?

On the "Members" page, click the "Advanced Search" button at the top. Click on the criteria you would like to filter by, and scroll down to the results. This will display a list of members who meet the desired criteria. From here, you can email these members or download a report of the members.


How do I add home group leaders or admin?

Go to the "Roles" tab. Find the user you want to make a home group leader or admin. If they do not already have an account, press create account. If they already have an account, press "Assign New Role" to give them a new role. If they already have a role, you can deactivate the previous role and assign a new one.
